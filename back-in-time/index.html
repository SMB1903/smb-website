Back in Time
Band

Founded by Doug Reece, the Back in Time Band features the most popular songs of the 20th century in that classic big band style, harkening back to the days of Tommy Dorsey and Glenn Miller.

The band is currently led by Davin Sooley, and coached by Andrea Lewis.
