Back in Time
Big Band

Founded by Doug Reece, The Back in Time Big Band performs and preserves pop music of the past, from the Big Band hits of the 1940s to the soul music of the 1960's and 70s.

The Back in Time Big Band and its predecessor, The Black Cats Orchestra, have entertained audiences in theatres, outdoor venues, dance halls, and wedding receptions all over southern New Brunswick.

The band is currently led by Davin Sooley and Andrea Lewis, and consists of Saint Mary's Band members.
